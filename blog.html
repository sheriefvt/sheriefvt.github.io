Learning using Gamification

Why gamification is effective in learning:

Gamification is the use of game design elements in non-game contexts, such as education. It can be an effective way to enhance learning because it:

  • Increases engagement and motivation
  • Provides immediate feedback
  • Makes learning more enjoyable
